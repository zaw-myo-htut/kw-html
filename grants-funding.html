Grants & Funding Support

Government support

Unlock funding for capability building and business innovation.

Singapore offers a range of grants to support SMEs in upgrading capabilities, adopting technology, and developing their workforce. Our approach

End-to-end grant application support

Pre-submission diagnosis

Our pre-submission diagnosis ensures only projects with high success probability proceed — saving you time and avoiding rejected applications.

Application preparation

We prepare comprehensive grant documentation aligned with Enterprise Singapore and SkillsFuture requirements, including project scopes, budgets, and outcomes.

Project delivery

As your appointed consultant, we deliver the funded project to scope — from IT implementation to workforce capability programmes — with full compliance reporting.

Claims & closure

We support milestone claims, audit documentation, and project closure to ensure smooth disbursement of approved grant funds.
