Our Story

From fixing tech issues to revolutionising how businesses harness technology.

Our story began when we assembled a team of like-minded tech experts and enthusiasts who realised a persistent issue in the industry — that is, many vendors were failing to deliver the right solutions to businesses.

Throughout our project history, we saw an opportunity to do more than just fix tech issues for businesses. We saw the potential to revolutionise the way businesses harness technology.

What we do

Comprehensive business solutions with an emphasis on digital and human capability.

We help businesses unlock new capabilities by providing comprehensive business solutions with an emphasis on both digital capabilities and human capability. Our work allows businesses of all sizes to transform themselves, weaving processes and technology to allow them to achieve more.

Having started off as the team to resolve deep technical issues arising from enterprise solutions provided by defunct or malicious vendors, KW Tech Solutions has evolved to incorporate process re-imagination and human capability building. Our dedicated consultants constantly strive to do more for our clients through expanding both our service offering and our methodology.

KW Tech Solutions endeavours to ensure that customers receive a solution just for them, engineered from both the business and technical standpoint.
